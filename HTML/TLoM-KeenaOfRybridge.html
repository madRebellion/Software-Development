This Life of Mine: Keena of Rybridge

I am Keena of Rybridge. Don't you forget it!

"They say the sword or axe should be an extension of your body to maximise your strength. If you need a weapon to be strong, then you are simply a weakling. Me? I don't need your trinkets. I am THE WEAPON!"

Who Am I?

Keena was born and raised in a small town called Rybridge. It is known in the region for its carpentry and excellent wooden sculpts.

Keena is the only child of Venn and Patch - with Venn the financial advisor to the mayor and Patch is an injured quartermaster - where she lived quite a spoilt life. Whatever she wanted, she got. Well... within reason of course. Keena was always cheerful, energetic (maybe a bit too energetic...) and brash which meant she often found herself in trouble for breaking things and almost (so very almost...) harming others around her. But she did it with a big smile and a cute little giggle! That's all that matter though right? The innocence of a child knows no bounds!

Fastforwarding to her late teenage years (16), Keena and her peers were gearing up to learn about the importance of self-defence and the world beyond the hills - a tradition in Rybridge - when it became painfully clear that Keena was not a good combatant. Keena couldn't quite grasp the basics of weapon holding and use, often taking an eternity just to swing the sword and forgetting about the shield entirely. Frustrated at this, Keena demands (from instructor Merkin) that she practice without the shield and bulky armour so that she can move more freely and with speed. Merkin denies this demand and reminds Keena that these pieces are designed to protect and help you survive if the time ever arose. Even more frustrated now, Keena forcefully throws her training gear onto the ground and proclaims that Instructor Merkin has lost touch with what it means to protect - remember Keena's father is a quatermaster - because of his time out of the Ryguards and boldly challenges Merkin to a simple fight. Keena is unarmed and unprotected here by the way. Merkin, again, rejects but Keena was already mid-punch. Keena made up so much ground in such a short period of time that not all the students realised she moved by the time she made physical contact with Merkin. And what punch it was. Merkin just can't react. He does eventually but it takes him a hot second or two to realise he was hit.

Red with rage, Keena was on the offensive, but not in control. Her feelings of uselessness, lack of freedom when fighting and overall anger have taken over her. She was not - and could not - thinking, only attacking. Keena was fighting hand-to-hand and armourless against a well-known decorated ex-guard using his signiture cross-long shield and polearm. But it seems like Keena has the upperhand. Not weighed down and only limited by her own speed, she is quite nippy it seems when she wants to be. But Merkin is done with this disobedience and swings his shield to knock Keena flying. Just one swing from this 'old man' was enough to end it. Oof, thats rough buddy. The attack takes a lot out of Keena but she's not done yet! Except she kinda is... Merkin demands that she stop and to spend more time practicing and maybe she will get the hang of it. "Practice makes perfect Keena! You won't master it first try!". Deflated and angry, Keena leaves but not without first promising that she will prove him wrong and that she can protect herself without the need for weapons. "I'll show you! I'll show all of you! I can do this by myself - and with my own way! I don't want your stupid sword beacuse I will be the weapon instead!"

She storms out and begins training by herself.
